R&D

With professional manpower and state-of-the-art analysis equipment, We, KG ETS, take the lead in the development of new products and diversified studies on the recovery of precious metals from waste, wastewater, and recycling, and technical development of new & renewable energy.

R&D

  • 01
    Wastes

    Optimization of waste and wastewater treatment. Applicaion of optimal methods in comprehensive consideration of legal requirements, health, safety, and environment through our systematic research and analysis for each type of hazardous waste and wastewater depending on their properties.

  • 02
    Wastewater

    Development of resources recovery processes. Incessant research and development to select optimally recyclable raw materials and improve goods' quality, and development of new products to study on value creation as inexhaustible resources.

  • 03
    Recycling

    Technology of new & renewable energy. Development of eco-rfiendly systems and waste-to-energy facilities for a resource circulation society.
